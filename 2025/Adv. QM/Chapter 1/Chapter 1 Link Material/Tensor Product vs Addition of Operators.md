1. Tensor Product: ($A\otimes B$) Acts Simultaneously 
	$A \in L(H), v \in H$
	$B \in L(H'), w \in H'$ 
	$(A\otimes B)(v \otimes w) = (Av)\otimes (Bw)$
	eg: Spin Orbit Coupling: $\sum_{i=1}^{3} L^i \otimes S^i$

2. Sum of Operators: ($A + B$) Acts Separately
	$A+B = A \otimes \mathbb{1}_{H'} + \mathbb{1}_H \otimes B$ 
	Use case: $(A+B)(v\otimes w) = (A \otimes \mathbb{1}_{H'} + \mathbb{1}_H \otimes B) (v\otimes w) = Av \otimes w + v \otimes Bw$  
	eg: Total momentum of two different particles $\vec{p}_{tot}=\vec{p}_{1}\otimes 1 + 1 \otimes \vec{p}_{2}$ 
	$\equiv i\hbar\vec{\nabla}_1 \otimes 1+1\otimes i\hbar\vec{\nabla}_2$
